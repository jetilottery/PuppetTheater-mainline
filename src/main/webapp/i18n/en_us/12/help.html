The Lucky Forest

Are you ready to play a Pick 8 game? The Lucky Forest gives you 8 opportunities to uncover animals for each ticket.

Try to get animals with an instant win to win the associated prize. If you get a bonus symbol, your get a secondary chance to uncover a bonus prize.

How to Play

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

[top]

Auto Play / Stop

Press AUTO PLAY to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until no opportunity left. When AUTO PLAY button is pressed, it will be replaced by STOP button. Players can press STOP to stop automatically playing. When STOP button is pressed, it will turn back to AUTO PLAY button.

[top]

About The Game

The object of the game is to uncover an Instant Win or a bonus symbol to win the associated prize.

Game Screen

After a ticket is purchased, the game displays a 8-chances ticket.

Winning a Prize

Click animals or press Auto Play button to reveal the animals. If an instant win is revealed, the associated prize will be awarded. If a bonus symbol is revealed, and a bonus amount greater than $0, the player wins the corresponding amount.

The total win will be the sum of all winning prize.

Game Completion

The game ends when 0 opportunity remain. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.
